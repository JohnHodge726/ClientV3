Who am I?

Qualified and professional DJ/MC with 4+ years of experience DJing weddings, proms, corporate parties, and other special events with DG Visionaries LLC, as well as an entry-level Producer/Audio Engineer with 2 years of experience producing, mixing and mastering music in a studio setting.

Born and raised in Elkhart, IN, I found a deep connection to music since joining band in the 6th grade at Concord Community Schools as well as atheltics with cross country, swimming and track. Upon graduating, I continued my love for music and running at Purdue University Fort Wayne where I earned a degree in Recording and Production and Music Industry as well as running D1 cross country and track.

I'm a curious, motivated professional recognized by my peers for my effective creative thinking, outgoing personality and interpersonal skills. Eagerly seeking to continue cultivating new experiences/relationships and gaining more knowledge of the art of Music Production in hopes of creating music of my own that I can one day combine with my love for DJing.
